# WarChess Monetization Strategy

## Helldivers 2-Inspired "Field Manual" System

> **Last updated:** 2026-04-04
> **Core principle:** No pay-to-win. All gameplay-affecting content (units, officers, commanders, formations) is earned exclusively through play.

---

## 1. Overview

This document redesigns WarChess's monetization around the Helldivers 2 Warbond model: permanent, non-expiring themed content packs with free and premium tracks, generous free earning of premium currency, and a no-FOMO philosophy.

### Naming (Napoleonic Theme)

| Helldivers 2 | WarChess Equivalent | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Warbond | **Field Manual** | Themed content pack with free + premium tracks |
| Super Credits | **Sovereigns** | Premium cosmetic currency (gold sovereign coins) |
| Medals | **Battle Stars** | Progression currency for unlocking Field Manual items |
| Rotating Store | **Quartermaster's Shop** | Daily rotating cosmetic shop |

## 2. Currency System

### Two Currencies (Intentional Separation)

| Currency | Purpose | Pay-to-Win Risk |
|---|---|---|
| **Sovereigns** | Cosmetic purchases, Field Manual premium tracks, Quartermaster's Shop | None (cosmetics only) |
| **Ammunition** | Multiplayer army deployments (gameplay convenience) | None (doesn't improve armies) |

These currencies are **never** convertible between each other. This prevents any pay-to-win pathway through Ammunition's deployment function.

### Earning Sovereigns for Free

| Source | Amount | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| Campaign battle first-clear | 5 | Per battle (30 battles = 150 total) |
| Multiplayer tier promotion | 2 | Per tier (5 tiers = 10 total) |
| Gold Dispatch Box drops | 1-3 | Random |
| 3-star all battles in a Campaign Act | 10 | Per act (3 acts = 30 total) |
| Day 7 consecutive login | 5 | Weekly |
| **Active player monthly earn** | **~60-80** | Ongoing |

A premium Field Manual costs 1,000 Sovereigns. Free players can earn one every ~3-4 months through dedicated play. This mirrors Helldivers 2's generous-but-slow pace.

### Purchasing Sovereigns (IAP)

| Pack | Price | Bonus |
|---|---|---|
| 100 Sovereigns | $0.99 | -- |
| 500 Sovereigns | $3.99 | -- |
| 1,200 Sovereigns | $7.99 | 20% bonus |
| 2,500 Sovereigns | $14.99 | 25% bonus |

## 3. Field Manual System

### Structure

Each Field Manual is themed around a Napoleonic campaign or theater of war. It contains:
- **4 pages** (unlock tiers)
- **Free track:** 6-8 items available to all players
- **Premium track:** 8-10 additional exclusive items (costs 1,000 Sovereigns to unlock)
- **~23 total items** per manual

Players spend **Battle Stars** to unlock items sequentially within each page.

### Earning Battle Stars

| Source | Stars | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| Campaign battle completed | 1 | Repeatable daily |
| Multiplayer win | 1 | Per win |
| Tier promotion | 2 | Per promotion |
| 3-day login streak | 1 | Per streak |
| Weekly challenge completed | 3 | 3 challenges/week |

### Page Breakdown

| Page | Items | Stars Per Item | Total Stars | Content Tier |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Page 1 | 3 | 5 | 15 | Common/Uncommon cosmetics |
| Page 2 | 3 | 8 | 24 | Uncommon + Ammunition grants |
| Page 3 | 4 | 12 | 48 | Rare + Sovereigns + Dispatch Box |
| Page 4 | 3 | 15 | 45 | Epic capstone cosmetics |
| **Total** | **13** | | **132** | ~26 days at 5 Stars/day |

### No Expiration (Core Principle)

**Field Manuals NEVER expire.** Once released, they remain permanently available. Players can purchase and progress through any manual at any time. No FOMO.

### Premium Track Value

Premium tracks return ~150-200 Sovereigns through embedded rewards, reducing the effective cost from 1,000 to ~800 Sovereigns. This rewards engagement and makes the purchase feel more generous.

### Content Types in Field Manuals

All cosmetic-only:
- Unit Skins (themed regiment variants)
- Grid Themes (battlefield visuals)
- Commander Portraits (alternative historical artwork)
- Victory Animations (themed celebrations)
- Army Banners (national flags, regimental standards)
- Officer Portrait Frames (NEW type -- decorative frames around officer icons)
- Ammunition grants (small amounts, convenience)
- Sovereign grants (partially offsets premium cost)
- Dispatch Boxes (Bronze in free track, Silver/Gold in premium)
- Battle Star Boosters (24h 2x earning, premium track only)

**NEVER in Field Manuals:** Officers, Commanders, unit types, formations, terrain, or any gameplay-affecting content.

## 4. Changes to Existing Systems

### Dispatch Boxes
- **No longer sold directly for real money.** Silver and Gold box IAP products removed.
- Still earned through gameplay (tier promotions, campaign achievements, season rewards)
- Appear as rewards within Field Manual tracks (both free and premium)
- More transparent: players can see exactly where boxes are in the Field Manual

### Cosmetic Shop -> Quartermaster's Shop
- Daily rotation continues (6 items, seeded RNG)
- Items priced in **Sovereigns** instead of Ammunition
- Field Manual-exclusive items never appear in the shop
- Sovereign prices: Common=50, Uncommon=100, Rare=250, Epic=500

### Ammunition System
- **Unchanged.** Still 3 free/day, still purchasable in existing packs.
- No longer used for cosmetic purchases (Sovereigns take over)
- Pure gameplay-convenience currency

### FOMO Policy
- Field Manuals: **Permanent** (never expire)
- Ranked season rewards (banners, titles, badges): **Season-exclusive** as prestige items
- This creates healthy aspiration for competitive players without predatory pressure

## 6. Competitive Pricing Analysis

### Battle Pass Benchmarks

| Game | Pass Price | Premium Currency | Content Price |
|---|---|---|---|
| Clash Royale | $4.99/month | Gems ($4.99 = 500) | -- |
| TFT | $9.90/~2 months | RP ($5 = 650) | -- |
| Marvel Snap | $9.99/month | Gold ($4.99 = 500) | -- |
| Helldivers 2 | $9.99/warbond (permanent) | SC ($3.99 = 500) | $39.99 base |
| **WarChess** | **~$7.99/manual (permanent)** | **Sov ($3.99 = 500)** | **$4.99 campaign** |

### Why $7.99 Field Manuals Work
- Below Helldivers 2 ($9.99) and Marvel Snap ($9.99)
- Appropriate for mobile-first audience with lower spending thresholds
- Permanent availability justifies slightly lower price vs time-limited passes
- Returns ~150-200 Sovereigns, making effective cost even lower

### Campaign at $4.99
- Competitive with premium mobile games (Bad North $4.99, Polytopia $4.99-$7.99)
- Clear value: 30 battles for "less than a coffee"
- Consider $2.99 sale during Act IV launch to drive conversions

## 10. Anti-Pay-to-Win Guarantees

1. **Units** unlock only through campaign progression or multiplayer tier wins
2. **Tier progression** requires actual wins (cannot be purchased)
3. **Sovereigns** cannot buy gameplay advantages (only cosmetics)
4. **Ammunition** does not improve armies (only deployment count)
5. **Officers and Commanders** are earned through play, never sold
6. **Field Manuals** contain zero gameplay-affecting content
7. **Multiplayer armies** are server-validated (budget, unit tiers, placement)
8. **Free earning** is generous enough that casual players never need to spend
