## 4. Full Description -- Apple App Store

```
WARCHESS -- NAPOLEONIC AUTO-BATTLER

Build your army. Set your formation. Watch the battle unfold.

WarChess is a tactical auto-battler set in the Napoleonic era. Command historically inspired units on a 10x10 grid, craft the perfect formation, and let the battle resolve itself. Victory goes to the player who out-thinks their opponent -- not the one who clicks fastest.

Every decision happens before the fighting starts. Choose your units, place them on the battlefield, assign Officers, and select your Commander. Once you confirm your deployment, the battle plays out automatically. Your strategy is your weapon.


FEATURES

-- 14 UNIT TYPES --
From steadfast Line Infantry and devastating Artillery to elite Old Guard and unpredictable Rocket Batteries. Each unit has unique stats, targeting behavior, and tactical role. Build balanced armies or go all-in on a single strategy.

-- 30-BATTLE CAMPAIGN --
Three acts of hand-crafted battles with escalating difficulty. Face entrenched defenses, cavalry ambushes, and artillery barrages. Unlock new units as you progress. Act 1 is completely free.

-- 6 LEGENDARY COMMANDERS --
Lead your army under Wellington, Napoleon, Kutuzov, Blucher, Moore, or Ney. Each Commander grants unique army-wide abilities that shape how your forces fight.

-- 12 OFFICERS WITH A TWIST --
Attach Officers to individual units for powerful bonuses -- but every Officer carries a drawback. The Veteran Sergeant hits harder but moves slower. The Reckless Captain charges devastatingly but crumbles under return fire. Every choice is a trade-off.

-- 8 TERRAIN TYPES & 5 FORMATIONS --
Forests, hills, fortifications, and more change the shape of every battle. Arrange your troops into Battle Lines, Cavalry Wedges, and Infantry Squares to unlock formation bonuses.

-- ASYNC MULTIPLAYER --
Build armies and submit them to the army pool. Your forces fight other players' armies automatically. Climb five Star General tiers, earn your rank, and prove your strategic mastery.

-- PIXEL ART STYLE --
Charming pixel art brings the Napoleonic battlefield to life with detailed unit sprites, terrain, and battle animations.

-- FAIR PLAY, ALWAYS --
No pay-to-win. No paying to unlock units faster. All 14 unit types are available in multiplayer from day one. The only premium purchase is the full campaign (Acts 2-3) for a single, one-time price.


PLAY YOUR WAY

Short sessions: A single battle takes 2-5 minutes -- perfect for commutes and lunch breaks. Long sessions: Chain campaign battles, experiment with army builds, and refine your multiplayer compositions.

Whether you love chess, auto-battlers, or historical strategy, WarChess offers deep tactical decisions in a beautifully compact package.

Build your army. Set your formation. Make history.
```

**Rationale:** WarChess features stylized pixel art combat between military units. There is no blood, gore, realistic violence, or depictions of death beyond units disappearing from the battlefield. The Napoleonic setting is historical but abstracted -- no real battles or atrocities are depicted. There is no user-generated content, no chat functionality, and no social features beyond async army pool matchmaking.

**Notes for questionnaire completion:**
- Violence: Cartoon/fantasy only. Units attack and are removed from the grid. No blood or injury depiction.
- Language: None.
- Sexual content: None.
- Substances: None.
- Gambling: None. Dispatch Boxes are cosmetic/symbolic only and cannot be purchased with real money.
- User interaction: Async only. No direct messaging, chat, or live interaction between players.
- In-app purchases: One-time $4.99 campaign unlock. No recurring purchases, loot boxes, or randomized paid content.

## 8. Privacy Policy Notes

The following points should be addressed in the full privacy policy document (required for both App Store and Google Play submission):

**Data collected:**
- Account identifier (for multiplayer matchmaking and army pool)
- Saved army compositions (synced for multiplayer)
- Star General tier and match history (for ranking)
- Device type and OS version (for crash reporting and compatibility)
- Anonymous analytics (session length, battles played, campaign progress) if analytics SDK is integrated

**Data NOT collected:**
- Real name, email, or phone number (unless account creation requires it)
- Location data
- Contacts, photos, or microphone access
- Health or financial data

**Third-party services:**
- List any analytics SDK (e.g., Unity Analytics, Firebase)
- List any crash reporting SDK (e.g., Crashlytics)
- List any ad SDK if free tier includes ads (currently: none planned)

**Children's privacy (COPPA / GDPR-K):**
- If targeting under-13 users (given E / PEGI 7 rating), ensure compliance with COPPA (US) and GDPR Article 8 (EU). Consider whether parental consent is required for data collection.
- If no data is collected from users under 13, state this explicitly.

**Apple App Tracking Transparency (ATT):**
- If no cross-app tracking is performed, no ATT prompt is required. State this in the privacy nutrition label.

**Google Play Data Safety:**
- Complete the Data Safety form listing all data types collected, purposes, and whether data is shared with third parties.
